Tech Squad Main Page

The tech Squad is a group of students who are in charge of Chromebook repair for Mayfield High School. The group of individuals are also in a class where they will learn a variety of technology related skills. The ultimate goal for anyone in the squad is to service the community and learn enough about technology that they can seem like a wizard among peers.
